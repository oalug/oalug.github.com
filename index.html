Ogden-Area Linux User Group

Welcome to the Ogden Area Linux Users Group (OALUG). We are a group of students, professionals, and hobbyists who share a passion for Linux, and free and open source software. The group serves Weber, Davis, and Morgan counties. We welcome absolutely anyone who is interested in learning, teaching, and helping the local community.

Meetings

We meet on the last Wednesday of every month.
7:00 to 9:00 P.M.

Membership

Membership is open and free, as are our meetings. We will never charge to become a member, attend any meeting, or participate in the group.
